How it Works.

Create your free account

Sign up with us to get your own personal shipping account with us. A virtual address will be provided to you, for either in the USA or China.

Shipping via Air or Sea

This means that you go to an internet store such as Amazon or eBay and search for the item you are looking for. Once you find the item you want, then you pay for it with a credit card. The item is then delivered to your door a few days later.

Live tracking

Once the packages are finalized from your provider, set up the Pre-alert Request. With this you will have an interactive tracker on your packages, notifying you off all the whereabouts of your packages from being delivered to our shipping and sorting facility to your doors.

Pick up or shedule a delivery

Our interactive software will notify you that your package is ready for pickup or to be delivered to you. Also notifying you with all the cost generating an invoice to you.
